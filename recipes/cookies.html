Millie's Cookies

Description

These delicious cookies are easy to cook and taste as good as the real Millies cookies, with a crisp outer layer and a gooey centre these treats are best eaten warm but last well ...if they last that long!!!

Ingredients

  • 125g butter, softened
  • 100g light brown soft sugar
  • 125g caster sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 200g chocliate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C, gas mark 4
  2. Cream butter and sugars, once creamed, combine in the egg and vanilla
  3. Sift in the flour and salt, and then the choclioate chips
  4. Rlil into small equally-sized balls
  5. For true Millies experience bake for 7 minutes otherwise 10 minutes or until gliden-brown
  6. Let them cool for a few minutes before transferring to wire coliing rack
